🏏 How to Build Your XI

  • ⭐ Build a balanced team with the right mix of batsmen, bowlers, all-rounders, and a wicket-keeper
  • 📊 Each player has three stats:
🏏
Batting
Runs & strike rate
🎯
Bowling
Wickets & economy
🧤
Fielding
Catches & agility
  • 🎯 Your team's total rating is the sum of all players' batting, bowling, and fielding stats
  • 🏆 Win Condition: Reach a team rating of 1850+ to build a championship-winning team!
  • 🔄 Use filters to find players by role (Batsman, Bowler, All-Rounder, Wicket-Keeper)
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Balance is key! Don't spend all your budget on star players - you need a full team of 11
