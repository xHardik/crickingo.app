🏏 How to Play

  • 🎯Match players to the correct category boxes in the grid
  • ✅Correct Answer: Earn +50 points and move to the next player
  • ❌Wrong Answer: Lose 10 points and skip the next player as penalty
  • ⏭️Skip: No points gained or lost, move to next player
  • 🔥Build Streaks: Get bonus points for consecutive correct answers!
  • 🏆Fill all 16 squares to complete the game and earn accuracy bonuses!
