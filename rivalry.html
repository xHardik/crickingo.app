🏏 How to Play

  • 🎯Match players to the correct category boxes in the grid
  • ✅Correct: Earn +50 points and move to the next player
  • ❌Wrong: Lose 10 points — next player is skipped as penalty
  • ⏭️Skip: No points lost, move on freely
  • 🔥Build Streaks: Consecutive correct answers earn bonus points!
  • 🏆Fill all 16 squares for accuracy bonuses!
