📖 How to Play

Welcome to Crickingo! Here's everything you need to know to get started with our cricket games.

Getting Started

  1. Select a game from the main menu
  2. Choose to play today's puzzle or select a specific date
  3. Follow the game-specific instructions
  4. Track your progress and compete with friends!

🔥 Cricket Bingo

Objective: Complete rows, columns, or diagonals based on CSK vs MI rivalry facts.

  • Read each square's challenge or question
  • Mark squares that match the criteria
  • First to complete a line wins!

🔄 Transfer History

Objective: Identify the cricket player based on their IPL team journey.

  • View the teams a player has played for
  • Use hints like years and positions
  • Guess the player's name within limited attempts

🔤 Player Wordle

Objective: Guess the cricket player's name in 6 attempts.

  • Enter a player name
  • Green letters are correct and in the right position
  • Yellow letters are in the name but wrong position
  • Gray letters are not in the name

📊 Higher or Lower

Objective: Guess if the next player's stat is higher or lower.

  • Compare cricket statistics between two players
  • Guess if the second player's stat is higher or lower
  • Keep your streak going as long as possible!

🧠 Build Your XI

Objective: Select 11 players that meet specific criteria.

  • Read the constraints (budget, team limits, etc.)
  • Choose players strategically
  • Create a balanced team within the rules

🎮 Play with Friends

Objective: Compete with friends in real-time tournaments.

  • Create a private room or join an existing one
  • Share the room code with friends
  • Compete and climb the tournament leaderboard

Tips for Success

  • Play daily to improve your cricket knowledge
  • Learn from incorrect guesses
  • Follow cricket news to stay updated
  • Join our community for tips and strategies
  • Challenge friends to beat your scores

Scoring

Each game has its own scoring system:

  • Speed bonus: Faster solutions earn more points
  • Accuracy: Fewer attempts mean higher scores
  • Streaks: Consecutive wins multiply your score

Ready to play? Head back to the home page and start your cricket journey! 🏏
