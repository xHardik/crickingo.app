🔄 How to Play

  • 📜Look at a player's IPL transfer history (teams & years)
  • 🔍Search and select the player's name from the dropdown
  • ✅Submit your guess to see if you're correct
  • 🔥Build a streak by guessing correctly in a row for bonus points!
  • ⏭️Skip if you don't know (but you'll lose your streak)
  • 🏆Guess all 3 players correctly to maximise your score!

2nd Correct (Streak Bonus)+300 pts
3rd Correct (Streak Bonus)+350 pts
Perfect Completion (3/3)+100 pts
