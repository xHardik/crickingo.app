Our Story

Crickingo was born out of a simple frustration — there weren't enough ways for cricket fans to engage with the sport they loved outside of match days. We wanted something you could play every morning with your chai, debate with friends, and come back to daily.

We started with a single game idea — a cricket-themed Wordle — and the response from the community blew us away. Within weeks, fans were asking for more: stat challenges, team builders, rivalry showdowns. So we built them. Every game on Crickingo exists because the community asked for it.

Today, Crickingo is home to 6+ games, new daily puzzles, and a growing community of cricket fans who believe that knowing your sport is just as exciting as watching it.

Our IPL Focus

We've gone deep on the Indian Premier League — and for good reason. IPL is one of the most data-rich, story-rich, and emotionally charged cricket competitions in the world. Player transfers, franchise loyalties, record-breaking innings — there's an endless well of content to draw from.
