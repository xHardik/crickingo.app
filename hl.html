⬆️⬇️ How to Play

  • 🎯 Compare cricket stats between two players
  • 👀 The left player's stat is shown
  • 🤔 Guess if the right player's stat is HIGHER or LOWER
  • 🔥 Build streaks for bonus points!
  • ❌ One wrong guess and it's game over
  • 🏆 Survive as long as possible to maximize your score

💰 Scoring System

• Base: 100 points per correct answer
• Streak Bonus: +20% per consecutive correct
Examples:
1st correct: 100 pts
2nd correct: 120 pts
3rd correct: 144 pts
4th correct: 173 pts
5th correct: 207 pts
Keep your streak alive for exponential growth!
